Genshin Impact: An Extremely Brief Guide

Official Version 3.0 Trailer Image. The person is named the Wanderer, formerly Scaramouche.

Genshin Impact is a game created by Hoyoverse (originally called Mihoyo) in late 2020. All content on this site belongs to Hoyoverse and their publisher company Cognosphere. The story focuses on two travelers that are separated after fighting an unknown god. Depending on who you choose to play with, you then embark on a journey within the world of Tevyat, hopefully to find your lost sibling. On your quest, you meet your new flying traveling companion, Paimon! You then engage with a wide variety of characters (many that you befriend and continue to see through further storylines and events), enemies, bosses, societal conflicts, and explore what new lands the game has to offer.

The Travelers

Both travlers fighting the unknown god. Left: Lumine. Right: Aether.

The image is an intro scene to the game where both travelers fight an unknown god. As just stated, you are given the choice on who to play with, and depending on your choice, you search for your other sibling. You then land in Tevyat to embark on your journey.

We find out more about the travlers throughout our story.
